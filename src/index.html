Hex Shaper

Help

This is a browser game simulating the Hex Shaper puzzle, a flat twisty puzzle manufactured by Very Puzzle, see their Product Page.

There are three overlapping disks with 12 outer pieces each, and one center piece. The disks can be rotated, exchanging the pieces. After scrambling, the goal is to bring them back to their original position.

To rotate a disk, just click on it. It rotates clockwise when you click on the right side, otherwise anti-clockwise. On desktop, keyboard navigation is also supported: The disks can be selected with the Tab key and rotated with the arrow keys.

The code is on GitHub.
